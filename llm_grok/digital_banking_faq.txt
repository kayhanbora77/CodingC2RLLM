Q: How do I reset my mobile banking password?
A: Go to login screen → Forgot Password → Enter registered mobile number → OTP will be sent → Set new password.

Q: Is UPI available 24/7?
A: Yes, UPI transactions are available 24×7 including holidays.

Q: What is the daily UPI limit?
A: ₹1,00,000 per day for most accounts (subject to bank policy).

Q: How to register for internet banking?
A: Visit branch or use mobile app → Registration → Enter account number, debit card details and set credentials.
